# AWS — resources

> roadmap.sh: https://roadmap.sh/aws

## Books
- **Amazon Web Services in Action** (Andreas & Michael Wittig) — hands-on, service-by-service intro that mirrors how the roadmap is structured.
- **AWS Certified Solutions Architect Study Guide** (Ben Piper & David Clinton) — broad coverage of core services with an architect's lens.
- **The Well-Architected Framework Whitepaper** (AWS) — the canonical mental model for reliability, security, cost, and performance trade-offs.
- **Cloud FinOps** (J.R. Storment & Mike Fuller) — the reference for the cost-management and tagging side of running AWS at scale.

## Courses / practice
- [AWS Skill Builder](https://skillbuilder.aws/) — AWS's own free training catalog, including the Cloud Practitioner and Solutions Architect paths.
- [AWS Documentation](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/) — the authoritative per-service reference; pair every node with its service docs.
- [AWS Workshops](https://workshops.aws/) — guided, hands-on labs for real architectures (serverless, containers, networking).
- [freeCodeCamp AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner course](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SOTamWNgDKc) — full-length video walkthrough of the foundational services.
