# Scala — resources

> roadmap.sh: https://roadmap.sh/scala

## Books
- **Programming in Scala** (Odersky, Spoon, Venners) — the definitive language book, from Scala's creator.
- **Functional Programming in Scala** (Chiusano & Bjarnason) — the "red book" for thinking functionally.
- **Scala with Cats** (Welsh & Gurnell) — practical type classes and FP patterns with Cats.
- **Hands-on Scala Programming** (Li Haoyi) — pragmatic, project-driven tour of the language and tooling.

## Courses / practice
- [Official Scala docs & tour](https://docs.scala-lang.org/) — canonical reference and the guided "Tour of Scala".
- [Scala 3 Book](https://docs.scala-lang.org/scala3/book/introduction.html) — free, official, modern intro.
- [Rock the JVM](https://rockthejvm.com/) — well-regarded video courses on Scala and its ecosystem.
- [Exercism Scala track](https://exercism.org/tracks/scala) — exercises with mentor feedback.
