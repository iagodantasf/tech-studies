# Laravel — resources

> roadmap.sh: https://roadmap.sh/laravel

## Books
- **Laravel: Up & Running** (Matt Stauffer) — the definitive guided tour of the framework's features and philosophy.
- **Battle Ready Laravel** (Ashley Allen) — auditing, testing, and hardening real Laravel apps for production.
- **Laravel Queues in Action** (Mohamed Said) — deep, practical coverage of queues, jobs, and background processing.
- **Refactoring to Collections** (Adam Wathan) — master Eloquent/Illuminate collections and a functional style in PHP.

## Courses / practice
- [Official Laravel docs](https://laravel.com/docs) — exceptionally well-written, the primary reference for every feature.
- [Laracasts](https://laracasts.com/) — the canonical Laravel video learning platform, made by the community's core educators.
- [Laravel Bootcamp](https://bootcamp.laravel.com/) — official hands-on guide building a real app with Blade, Livewire, and Inertia.
- [Laravel News](https://laravel-news.com/) — stay current with releases, packages, and ecosystem tutorials.
