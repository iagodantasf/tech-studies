# Terraform — resources

> roadmap.sh: https://roadmap.sh/terraform

## Books
- **Terraform: Up & Running** (Yevgeniy Brikman) — the definitive practical guide to modules, state, and team workflows.
- **The Terraform Book** (James Turnbull) — approachable hands-on introduction from first resource to real infrastructure.
- **Infrastructure as Code** (Kief Morris) — the broader IaC principles and patterns that Terraform implements.

## Courses / practice
- [Terraform official documentation](https://developer.hashicorp.com/terraform/docs) — authoritative reference for the language, CLI, and workflow.
- [HashiCorp Terraform tutorials](https://developer.hashicorp.com/terraform/tutorials) — official guided, provider-specific learning paths.
- [Terraform Registry](https://registry.terraform.io/) — providers and reusable community modules.
- [Terraform Associate certification prep](https://developer.hashicorp.com/terraform/tutorials/certification-003) — structured study path covering the exam objectives.
