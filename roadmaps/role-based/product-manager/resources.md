# Product Manager — resources

> roadmap.sh: https://roadmap.sh/product-manager

## Books
- **Inspired** (Marty Cagan) — the definitive guide to how the best teams build products customers love.
- **The Lean Startup** (Eric Ries) — build-measure-learn, MVPs, and validated learning under uncertainty.
- **Continuous Discovery Habits** (Teresa Torres) — a practical framework for weekly customer touchpoints and opportunity solution trees.
- **Escaping the Build Trap** (Melissa Perri) — how to shift from shipping features to delivering measurable value.

## Courses / practice
- [Lenny's Newsletter](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/) — the most widely read source of practical PM craft, growth, and career advice.
- [Reforge](https://www.reforge.com/) — advanced programs on growth, product strategy, and monetization from operators.
- [SVPG articles](https://www.svpg.com/articles/) — Marty Cagan and Silicon Valley Product Group on modern product discovery and team models.
- [Product School blog](https://productschool.com/blog) — accessible articles, templates, and frameworks across the PM discipline.
