# Engineering Manager — resources

> roadmap.sh: https://roadmap.sh/engineering-manager

## Books
- **The Manager's Path** (Camille Fournier) — the canonical guide to engineering leadership from tech lead to CTO.
- **An Elegant Puzzle** (Will Larson) — systems thinking applied to org design, team sizing, and engineering management problems.
- **Radical Candor** (Kim Scott) — how to care personally while challenging directly to give feedback that lands.
- **The Making of a Manager** (Julie Zhuo) — a practical, honest field guide for new managers in their first year.

## Courses / practice
- [LeadDev articles](https://leaddev.com/) — conference talks and writing focused specifically on engineering leadership.
- [Will Larson — Irrational Exuberance](https://lethain.com/) — deep essays on engineering management, strategy, and org design.
- [The Pragmatic Engineer](https://blog.pragmaticengineer.com/) — Gergely Orosz on real-world engineering and management practices at scale.
- [Engineering Management resources (charity.wtf)](https://charity.wtf/) — Charity Majors on the IC/manager pendulum and modern leadership.
