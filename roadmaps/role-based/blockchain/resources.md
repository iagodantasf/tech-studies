# Blockchain — resources

> roadmap.sh: https://roadmap.sh/blockchain

## Books
- **Mastering Bitcoin** (Andreas Antonopoulos) — the definitive technical deep-dive into how Bitcoin works under the hood.
- **Mastering Ethereum** (Antonopoulos & Wood) — companion volume covering Ethereum, the EVM and smart contracts in depth.
- **The Basics of Bitcoins and Blockchains** (Antony Lewis) — accessible, jargon-light overview of the whole ecosystem.
- **Token Economy** (Shermin Voshmgir) — clear explanation of tokens, Web3 and decentralized organizations.

## Courses / practice
- [Solidity docs](https://docs.soliditylang.org/) — the official language reference and the source of truth for smart contract development.
- [CryptoZombies](https://cryptozombies.io/) — gamified, hands-on course that teaches Solidity by building a game.
- [Ethereum.org developer portal](https://ethereum.org/en/developers/) — official docs, tutorials and tooling for building on Ethereum.
- [Foundry Book](https://book.getfoundry.sh/) — official guide to the Foundry toolchain for testing and deploying contracts.
