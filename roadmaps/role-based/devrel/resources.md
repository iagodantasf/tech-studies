# Developer Relations — resources

> roadmap.sh: https://roadmap.sh/devrel

## Books
- **The Business Value of Developer Relations** (Mary Thengvall) — the foundational book on why DevRel exists and how to measure it.
- **Developer Marketing and Relations: The Essential Guide** (SlashData / Caroline Lewko & James Parton) — practical playbook for reaching and growing developer audiences.
- **The Developer's Guide to Content Creation** (Stephanie Morillo) — concrete, repeatable process for shipping technical content.
- **Trust Me, I'm Lying** (Ryan Holiday) — sharp view of how media and attention actually work; useful skepticism for any advocacy role.

## Courses / practice
- [DevRel Collective](https://devrelcollective.fun/) — the largest community of DevRel practitioners; Slack, resources, and shared playbooks.
- [Write the Docs](https://www.writethedocs.org/) — the canonical community and guide for documentation and technical writing.
- [CommonMark](https://commonmark.org/help/) — learn the Markdown you'll write docs and posts in every day.
- [Speaking.io](https://speaking.io/) — Zach Holman's practical guide to preparing and delivering conference talks.
